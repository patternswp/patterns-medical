Patterns Medical is a clean and professional block-based WordPress theme designed for clinics, hospitals, doctors, and healthcare providers. It offers all the essential tools to create a reliable and informative online presence for medical services.

== Description ==

Patterns Medical is a modern and purpose-driven Full Site Editing (FSE) WordPress theme, ideal for building a professional online presence for clinics, hospitals, medical centers, and healthcare professionals. Powered by WordPress Full Site Editing, this theme enables effortless customization of headers, footers, templates, and global styles through the intuitive Site Editor.

It comes with a collection of pre-designed patterns and layouts tailored for healthcare services, doctor profiles, patient testimonials, treatment information, pricing sections, contact forms, and appointment bookings. The fully responsive design ensures your website remains accessible, clean, and professional across all devices.

Whether you're managing a private clinic, multi-specialty hospital, or individual practice, Patterns Medical delivers a reliable and effective platform to build trust and connect with patients online.
